# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and harassment-free
environment for contributors and users.

## Expected behavior

- Be precise and constructive.
- Critique code and decisions, not people.
- Protect private and security-sensitive information.
- Do not post real credentials or private repository content.
- Respect maintainer decisions and documented project scope.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the primary maintainer. Maintainers may
edit or remove content, close contributions, or restrict participation when
needed to protect contributors or the project.
